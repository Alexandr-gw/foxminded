Musician, singer and record producer

Michael Kenji Shinoda (Japanese: 篠田 賢治, romanized: Shinoda Kenji; born February 11, 1977) - He co-founded the rock band Linkin Park, created a hip-hop-driven side project, Fort Minor, in 2004. Also served as a producer for tracks and albums by Lupe Fiasco, Styles of Beyond.
